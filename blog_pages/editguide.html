Photo/Film Editing Guide

My perspective on what adjustments on contrast, saturation, tone and colors you can make to your photo/films, to portray the feeling you want your work to give off.



Lower contrast, higher saturation

  • Generally gives vibrant, lively, "carefree" animated vibes.
  • The color richness may even portray an "anime-like" feel (e.g. Studio Ghibli scenery)
  • When neither very deep shadows nor strong highlights (low contrast) are present, the color richness present may give a 2D perspective.
